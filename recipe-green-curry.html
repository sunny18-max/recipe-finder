Thai

Green Curry

40 mins Serves 4 Medium
4.5 (298 reviews)

Fragrant Thai green curry with tender chicken, eggplant and bamboo shoots in a creamy coconut sauce. The perfect balance of spicy, sweet and savory flavors with a vibrant green color from fresh herbs.

Ingredients

For Curry:

  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2-3 tbsp green curry paste
  • 500g chicken thigh, sliced
  • 1 cup Thai eggplant, quartered
  • 1/2 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 6 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/2 cup Thai basil leaves

For Paste (or use store-bought):

  • 10 green chilies
  • 3 shallots
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 stalk lemongrass
  • 1 tsp galangal
  • 1 tsp coriander root
  • 1 tsp shrimp paste

Instructions

Step 1: Cook Paste

Heat 1/4 cup coconut milk in pot until bubbling. Add curry paste and fry 2-3 minutes until fragrant.

Step 2: Add Chicken

Add chicken and cook until no longer pink (about 5 minutes).

Step 3: Add Vegetables

Add remaining coconut milk, eggplant, bamboo shoots, bell pepper and lime leaves. Simmer 10 minutes.

Step 4: Season

Add fish sauce and palm sugar. Taste and adjust seasoning - should be slightly sweet, salty and spicy.

Step 5: Finish

Stir in Thai basil just before serving. Serve with jasmine rice.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Vegetarian Option: Substitute chicken with tofu and use vegetarian fish sauce.

Spice Level: Adjust chili amount in paste. Remove seeds for milder version.

Shortcut: Quality store-bought paste works well (Mae Ploy or Maesri brands recommended).

Traditional Touch: Add pea eggplants for authentic texture.
