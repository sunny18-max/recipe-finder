Medu Vada

45 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian
4.9 (412 reviews)

Crispy on the outside, soft and fluffy inside, these savory South Indian lentil donuts are a breakfast favorite. Made from urad dal batter seasoned with black pepper, cumin, and curry leaves, these golden-brown vadas are traditionally served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tbsp rice flour
  • 1 tsp whole black pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 10-12 curry leaves, chopped

For Frying:

  • Oil for deep frying

For Seasoning:

  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp coconut, grated (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Soak Dal

Wash and soak urad dal for 4-5 hours. Drain completely before grinding.

Step 2: Grind Batter

Grind dal to smooth fluffy batter with minimal water. Batter should be thick.

Step 3: Season Batter

Add rice flour, pepper, cumin, curry leaves, ginger, chilies, coconut and salt. Whip well.

Step 4: Shape Vadas

Wet hands, take lemon-sized batter, make hole in center with thumb.

Step 5: Fry

Deep fry in medium-hot oil until golden brown on both sides. Drain on paper towels.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Batter Tip: Whipping batter well makes vadas fluffy.

Consistency: Batter should be thick like cake batter.

Serving Suggestion: Best served hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Variation: Add finely chopped onions for onion vada.
