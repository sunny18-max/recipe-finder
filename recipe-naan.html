Garlic Naan

2 hours 30 mins Makes 8 Easy
4.6 (198 reviews)

Soft, pillowy naan bread brushed with garlic butter and cooked in a hot tandoor. This restaurant-style recipe gives you perfect naan at home, whether you use an oven, stovetop or grill.

Ingredients

For Dough:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp oil

For Garlic Butter:

  • 4 tbsp butter, melted
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped

Instructions

Step 1: Activate Yeast

Mix yeast, sugar and warm water. Let sit 10 minutes until frothy.

Step 2: Make Dough

Combine flour, salt, baking powder. Add yeast mixture, yogurt and oil. Knead 8-10 minutes until smooth.

Step 3: First Rise

Cover dough and let rise in warm place 1-2 hours until doubled.

Step 4: Shape Naan

Divide dough into 8 balls. Roll each into oval shape, about 1/4 inch thick.

Step 5: Cook

Cook on hot skillet 1-2 minutes per side until puffed and charred. Brush with garlic butter.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Yeast Substitute: Use 1 tsp baking soda + 1 tsp lemon juice if no yeast available.

Oven Method: Bake at 260°C (500°F) on pizza stone for 2-3 minutes.

Variations: Add nigella seeds, stuff with cheese, or make plain butter naan.

Storage: Best eaten fresh. Reheat in skillet or toaster to restore texture.
