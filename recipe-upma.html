Rava Upma

20 mins Serves 3 Vegetarian
4.3 (189 reviews)

Light and fluffy semolina breakfast dish cooked with vegetables and tempered with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and spices. A quick and nutritious morning meal.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 10-12 curry leaves

Vegetables:

  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/4 cup green peas

Other:

  • 2.5 cups water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander

Instructions

Step 1: Roast Rava

Dry roast semolina in a pan on low heat until fragrant (5-6 mins). Set aside.

Step 2: Prepare Tempering

Heat oil, add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal. When they splutter, add curry leaves, onions, ginger, green chili.

Step 3: Cook Vegetables

Add vegetables and saut√© for 2-3 mins. Add water and salt, bring to boil.

Step 4: Add Rava

Slowly add roasted semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Cover and cook on low heat for 3-4 mins.

Step 5: Finish

Turn off heat. Add lemon juice and coriander. Fluff with fork before serving.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Rava Quality: Use fine Bombay rava for best texture. Coarse rava requires more water and cooking time.

Vegetable Variations: Try with bell peppers, corn, or grated beetroot for color. For protein, add roasted peanuts or paneer cubes.

Quick Version: Skip vegetables and make plain upma with just onions and green chilies in 10 minutes.

Leftover Idea: Press cooled upma in plate, cut into squares, and shallow fry for crispy upma cakes.
