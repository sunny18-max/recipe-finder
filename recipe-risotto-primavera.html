Risotto Primavera

40 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian
4.5 (287 reviews)

A vibrant spring vegetable risotto bursting with seasonal produce like asparagus, peas, and zucchini. This colorful dish celebrates the flavors of spring with its light yet creamy texture and fresh herb finish, making it perfect for seasonal dining.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 320g carnaroli or arborio rice
  • 1.5 liters vegetable stock
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 120ml dry white wine

Spring Vegetables:

  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut
  • 1 cup fresh peas
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

For Finishing:

  • 60g butter
  • 60g parmesan, grated
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Vegetables

Blanch asparagus and peas in boiling water for 2 minutes, then shock in ice water. Sauté zucchini until just tender.

Step 2: Sauté Onion

In heavy pan, melt half the butter with olive oil. Cook onion until soft but not colored.

Step 3: Toast Rice

Add rice and stir to coat in fat. Cook 2 minutes until translucent at edges.

Step 4: Deglaze

Pour in wine and stir until absorbed. Begin adding hot stock one ladle at a time.

Step 5: Finish

When rice is almost done (15 mins), stir in vegetables. Off heat, stir in remaining butter, parmesan and fresh herbs. Season and serve immediately.

Chef's Notes

Seasonal Variations: Use whatever spring vegetables are freshest.

Herb Options: Mint or chives work well in place of basil.

Presentation: Garnish with edible flowers for spring aesthetic.

Make Ahead: Prepare vegetables in advance for quicker assembly.
