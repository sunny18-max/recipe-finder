Paneer Tikka

45 mins Serves 4 Medium
4.9 (287 reviews)

Cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in spiced yogurt and grilled to perfection. This vegetarian version of chicken tikka makes a fantastic appetizer or main course, bursting with smoky flavors from the tandoor.

Ingredients

For Marinade:

  • 400g paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil

For Vegetables:

  • 1 bell pepper, cubed
  • 1 onion, cubed
  • 1 tomato, deseeded

For Serving:

  • Lemon wedges
  • Mint chutney
  • Onion rings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Marinade

Whisk yogurt until smooth. Add all spices, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice and oil. Mix well.

Step 2: Marinate

Add paneer and vegetables to marinade. Gently mix. Refrigerate at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

Step 3: Skewer

Thread paneer and vegetables alternately on skewers. Reserve leftover marinade.

Step 4: Grill

Grill on high heat 3-4 minutes per side, basting with reserved marinade, until charred.

Step 5: Serve

Sprinkle chaat masala. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Paneer Tip: Use firm, fresh paneer. Soak in warm water 10 minutes to prevent drying.

No Grill Option: Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 15-20 minutes or pan-fry.

Flavor Boost: Add 1 tbsp besan (gram flour) to marinade for extra texture.

Vegan Option: Substitute paneer with tofu and use dairy-free yogurt.
