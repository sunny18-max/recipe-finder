Risotto alla Zucca

45 mins Serves 4 Medium
4.9 (243 reviews)

Creamy Italian risotto with sweet roasted pumpkin, aromatic sage, and nutty Parmesan cheese. This autumn favorite combines seasonal flavors with the perfect al dente texture of Carnaroli rice.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups Carnaroli or Arborio rice
  • 500g pumpkin, peeled and cubed
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Seasonings:

  • 6 fresh sage leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pinch of nutmeg (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Pumpkin

Roast pumpkin cubes at 200°C (400°F) for 20 minutes until tender. Mash half and keep half as cubes.

Step 2: Sauté Aromatics

In a large pan, sauté onion in butter and olive oil until translucent. Add sage leaves and cook for 1 minute.

Step 3: Toast Rice

Add rice and toast for 2 minutes until slightly translucent at edges. Deglaze with white wine.

Step 4: Cook Risotto

Add warm stock one ladle at a time, stirring constantly. After 15 minutes, add mashed pumpkin.

Step 5: Finish

When rice is al dente (about 18-20 minutes total), stir in pumpkin cubes, Parmesan, and adjust seasoning.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Rice Choice: Carnaroli is ideal, but Arborio works well too. Never rinse risotto rice.

Stock Temperature: Keep stock simmering nearby to maintain cooking temperature.

Texture Tip: Risotto should flow like lava (all'onda) when plated.

Variations: Add crispy pancetta or substitute butternut squash for pumpkin.
