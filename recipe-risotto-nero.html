Risotto al Nero di Seppia

50 mins Serves 4 Advanced
4.7 (176 reviews)

Dramatic black risotto infused with briny squid ink and tender pieces of fresh squid. A Venetian specialty that's as striking in presentation as it is delicious, with flavors of the sea in every bite.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups Carnaroli rice
  • 400g fresh squid, cleaned
  • 2 sachets (4g each) squid ink
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 4 cups fish stock
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine

Seasonings:

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 2 tbsp parsley, chopped

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Squid

Clean squid and separate tentacles from bodies. Cut bodies into rings and chop tentacles. Set aside.

Step 2: Sauté Aromatics

In a large pan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft. Add squid and cook for 2 minutes.

Step 3: Toast Rice

Add rice and toast for 2 minutes. Deglaze with white wine and let alcohol evaporate.

Step 4: Add Ink

Dissolve squid ink in 1/2 cup warm fish stock and add to rice along with first ladle of stock.

Step 5: Cook Risotto

Continue adding stock gradually, stirring often, for 18 minutes until rice is al dente.

Step 6: Finish

Remove from heat, stir in butter and parsley. Cover and let rest 2 minutes before serving.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Squid Ink: Available at specialty food stores or online. Freeze extra sachets for future use.

Stock Tip: Use homemade fish stock if possible for best flavor. Warm before adding.

Serving Suggestion: Garnish with lemon wedges and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.

Variation: Add peeled shrimp or scallops in the last 5 minutes of cooking.
