Rava Idli

25 mins Serves 4 Easy
4.5 (238 reviews)

Soft, fluffy instant idlis made with semolina (rava/sooji) instead of rice. These quick South Indian breakfast treats require no fermentation and can be made in minutes with our foolproof recipe.

Ingredients

For Batter:

  • 2 cups semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp fruit salt (eno)
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For Tempering:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • Few curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp cashews

For Garnish:

  • 2 tbsp grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Batter

Mix rava, yogurt and water to make thick batter. Let rest for 15 minutes.

Step 2: Temper

Heat oil. Add mustard seeds, dals, chili, ginger and curry leaves. Saut√© until golden. Add to batter.

Step 3: Add Leavening

Just before steaming, add fruit salt and 1 tbsp water. Mix gently.

Step 4: Steam

Grease idli molds. Pour batter and steam for 10-12 minutes.

Step 5: Serve

Garnish with coconut and coriander. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Variation: Add grated carrots or peas for vegetable rava idli.

Vegan Option: Replace yogurt with coconut milk or buttermilk.

Storage: Refrigerate batter for up to 2 days. Steam fresh when needed.

Pro Tip: Use fine rava for softer idlis.
