Vegetable Pakora

30 mins Serves 4 Easy
4.7 (89 reviews)

Crispy, golden vegetable fritters made with a spiced chickpea flour batter. These popular Indian street food snacks are perfect with mint chutney or tamarind sauce and make an irresistible appetizer or rainy-day snack.

Ingredients

For Batter:

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric

For Vegetables:

  • 1 potato, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Oil for deep frying

For Chutney:

  • 1 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 1 green chili
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

Step 1: Make Batter

Whisk together flours, spices and enough water to make thick pancake batter consistency.

Step 2: Prepare Vegetables

Mix all vegetables into batter until well coated.

Step 3: Fry Pakoras

Heat oil to 350Â°F. Drop spoonfuls of mixture into oil. Fry until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes.

Step 4: Make Chutney

Blend all chutney ingredients with salt to taste.

Chef's Notes

Dough Tip: The dough should be stiffer than roti dough for crisp samosas.

Frying Secret: Maintain medium heat - too hot will brown outside before inside cooks.

Variation: Try minced meat or paneer fillings for non-veg versions.

Storage: Freeze uncooked samosas for up to a month.
