Italian

Classic Tiramisu

6 hours (includes chilling) Serves 8 Contains Eggs
4.9 (892 reviews)

The iconic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and a rich, creamy mascarpone filling, dusted with cocoa powder. This no-bake dessert is light yet indulgent, with the perfect balance of coffee, cream, and sweetness that makes it a crowd favorite.

Ingredients

For the Coffee Mixture:

  • 1½ cups strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 2 tbsp sugar

For the Cream:

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese

For Assembly:

  • 24-30 ladyfinger cookies
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
  • Dark chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Coffee Mixture

Combine cooled coffee, liqueur (if using), and sugar in shallow dish. Stir until sugar dissolves.

Step 2: Make Zabaglione

Whisk egg yolks and sugar in heatproof bowl over simmering water until pale and thick (about 10 mins). Remove from heat.

Step 3: Whip Cream

In separate bowl, whip heavy cream to stiff peaks. Fold mascarpone into cooled egg mixture, then gently fold in whipped cream.

Step 4: Assemble Layers

Quickly dip ladyfingers in coffee mixture (don't soak) and arrange in single layer in 8x8" dish. Spread half the cream mixture over. Repeat layers.

Step 5: Chill and Serve

Cover and refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight. Before serving, dust with cocoa powder and chocolate shavings.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Egg Safety: Use pasteurized eggs if concerned about raw yolks.

Non-Alcoholic: Omit liqueur and use 2 tsp vanilla extract instead.

Make Ahead: Tastes even better after 24 hours chilling.

Presentation: Serve in individual glasses for elegant plating.
