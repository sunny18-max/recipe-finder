Mango Sticky Rice

1 hour 30 mins Serves 4 Easy
4.9 (412 reviews)

The ultimate Thai dessert featuring sweet sticky rice topped with luscious ripe mango and drizzled with rich coconut sauce. This heavenly combination of textures and flavors is surprisingly simple to make at home.

Ingredients

For Sticky Rice:

  • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • 1 3/4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4-5 pandan leaves (optional)

For Coconut Sauce:

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp rice flour (optional)

For Serving:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • 2 tbsp toasted mung beans (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Soak Rice

Rinse rice until water runs clear. Soak in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Step 2: Steam Rice

Drain rice. Line steamer with cheesecloth, add rice and pandan leaves. Steam 25-30 minutes until translucent.

Step 3: Make Coconut Sauce

Heat coconut milk, sugar and salt until sugar dissolves. Thicken slightly with rice flour if desired.

Step 4: Combine

Mix half the sauce with hot rice. Let sit 15 minutes to absorb flavors.

Step 5: Serve

Plate rice with mango slices. Drizzle remaining sauce. Garnish with mung beans and mint.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Mango Selection: Use ripe Ataulfo or Kent mangoes for best flavor and texture.

Rice Alternative: Can use regular sticky rice if glutinous rice unavailable.

Make Ahead: Rice can be prepared 1 day in advance and reheated with steam.

Vegan Option: Use coconut sugar instead of white sugar.
