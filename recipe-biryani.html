Chicken Biryani

1 hour 30 mins Serves 6 Main Course Medium Spice
4.7 (8,921 reviews)

Fragrant basmati rice layered with succulent marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and aromatic spices. This Hyderabadi-style biryani features the perfect dum cooking technique for maximum flavor infusion.

Ingredients

For Chicken Marinade:

  • 1.5 lbs chicken (thighs & drumsticks)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste

For Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked 30 mins)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp salt

For Layering:

  • 2 large onions (sliced & fried)
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 tsp saffron (soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk)
  • 2 tbsp fried cashews (garnish)

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate Chicken

Mix all marinade ingredients with chicken. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight preferred).

Step 2: Prepare Rice

Boil rice with whole spices until 70% cooked (about 5-6 mins). Drain and set aside.

Step 3: Cook Chicken

In a heavy pot, cook marinated chicken for 15 mins until partially done. Remove chicken, reserve gravy.

Step 4: Layering

In same pot: Layer half rice → chicken & gravy → herbs → fried onions → remaining rice → saffron milk → ghee. Cover tightly.

Step 5: Dum Cooking

Cook on low heat for 25 mins (place tawa below to prevent burning). Let rest 10 mins before serving with raita.

Chef's Notes

Rice Tip: Soaking basmati rice prevents breakage and ensures long grains.

Marination Time: Overnight marination yields more flavorful chicken.

Dum Technique: Seal pot lid with dough for authentic steam cooking.

Serving: Best paired with mirchi ka salan and onion raita.
