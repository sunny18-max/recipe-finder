Authentic Guacamole

15 mins Serves 4-6 Vegan
4.9 (512 reviews)

Creamy, perfectly seasoned avocado dip made with just a few fresh ingredients. This traditional Mexican guacamole recipe highlights the natural flavor of ripe avocados with the perfect balance of lime, cilantro, and a touch of heat.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe Hass avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely diced
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
  • 3 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped

For Heat (Optional):

  • 1 jalape√±o or serrano pepper, minced
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin

For Serving:

  • Tortilla chips
  • Radish slices
  • Extra lime wedges

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Avocados

Cut avocados in half, remove pits, and scoop flesh into molcajete (or bowl). Add lime juice and salt. Mash with pestle or fork to desired consistency.

Step 2: Add Aromatics

In separate bowl, mix onion, cilantro, and chili pepper. Let sit for 5 minutes to mellow onion flavor.

Step 3: Combine Ingredients

Gently fold onion mixture into mashed avocado. Add diced tomatoes and cumin. Taste and adjust seasoning.

Step 4: Serve Immediately

Transfer to serving dish. Place avocado pit in center to help prevent browning. Serve with chips.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Avocado Selection: Choose avocados that yield slightly to gentle pressure at stem end.

Traditional Method: For authentic texture, use molcajete (lava stone mortar and pestle).

Variations: Add diced mango or pomegranate seeds for sweet twist.

No Browning Trick: Keep pit in guacamole and cover surface with lime juice before refrigerating.
