Homemade Sushi Rolls

1 hour Serves 2-3 Medium
4.8 (215 reviews)

Learn to make beautiful sushi rolls at home with this step-by-step guide. Fresh ingredients, perfectly seasoned rice, and professional rolling techniques will have you creating restaurant-quality maki rolls in no time!

Ingredients

For Sushi Rice:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

For California Rolls:

  • 5 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 8 oz imitation crab meat
  • Sesame seeds

For Spicy Tuna Rolls:

  • 1/2 lb fresh tuna, diced
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

For Serving:

  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Rice

Rinse rice until water runs clear. Cook with water, then transfer to wooden bowl. Mix vinegar, sugar and salt, then fold into rice while fanning to cool. Cover with damp cloth.

Step 2: Prepare Fillings

Slice all vegetables into thin matchsticks. For spicy tuna, mix diced tuna with mayo, sriracha and sesame oil.

Step 3: Roll Sushi

Place nori on bamboo mat. Spread thin layer of rice, leaving 1-inch border. Add fillings in center. Roll tightly using mat, moistening edge to seal. Slice with wet knife.

Step 4: Serve

Arrange on platter with soy sauce, wasabi and ginger. For inside-out rolls, press sesame seeds onto rice exterior before slicing.

Chef's Notes

Rice Tip: Use wooden or plastic utensils for sushi rice - metal can affect flavor. Keep rice covered to prevent drying.

Rolling Trick: Apply gentle, even pressure when rolling. Don't overfill - about 1/4 of nori should be filling.

Variations: Try tempura shrimp, smoked salmon, or vegetarian with mango and cream cheese.

Safety: Use sushi-grade fish from reputable sources if making raw fish rolls.
