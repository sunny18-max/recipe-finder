Chicken Enchiladas

1 hour Serves 6 Medium
4.6 (428 reviews)

Tender shredded chicken wrapped in corn tortillas, smothered in red enchilada sauce and melted cheese. These authentic Mexican enchiladas are baked to perfection with layers of flavor from roasted tomatoes, garlic, and Mexican spices.

Ingredients

For Filling:

  • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro

For Sauce:

  • 6 dried ancho chilies
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp oregano

For Assembly:

  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 3 cups shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup Mexican crema
  • 1 avocado, sliced

Instructions

Step 1: Make Sauce

Toast dried chilies, then simmer with tomatoes and broth for 15 minutes. Blend until smooth and strain.

Step 2: Prepare Filling

Sauté onion and garlic, add chicken and spices. Mix in 1/2 cup of the enchilada sauce.

Step 3: Assemble

Dip tortillas in warm sauce, fill with chicken mixture, roll and place seam-side down in baking dish.

Step 4: Bake

Pour remaining sauce over enchiladas, top with cheese. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes until bubbly.

Step 5: Garnish

Drizzle with crema, top with avocado slices and fresh cilantro before serving.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Chili Tip: For milder flavor, remove seeds from dried chilies before toasting.

Shortcut: Use rotisserie chicken and store-bought enchilada sauce.

Vegetarian Option: Substitute chicken with black beans and roasted vegetables.

Key Tip: Warm tortillas before rolling to prevent cracking.
