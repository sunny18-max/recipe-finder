Authentic Street Tacos

1 hour Serves 4 Medium
4.7 (189 reviews)

Soft corn tortillas filled with juicy marinated carne asada, topped with fresh cilantro, onions, and a squeeze of lime. These authentic street-style tacos will transport you straight to the streets of Mexico City!

Ingredients

For the Carne Asada:

  • 2 lbs flank or skirt steak
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

For Serving:

  • 16 small corn tortillas
  • 1 white onion, finely diced
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges
  • Salsa verde or roja
  • Radish slices (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Meat

Combine all marinade ingredients in a bowl. Place steak in a ziplock bag or shallow dish, pour marinade over, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (up to 8 hours for best flavor).

Step 2: Grill the Steak

Heat grill or cast iron skillet to high heat. Remove steak from marinade and grill for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing against the grain into thin strips.

Step 3: Prepare Tortillas

Heat tortillas on a dry skillet or directly over gas flame for about 30 seconds per side until slightly charred but still pliable. Keep warm wrapped in a clean kitchen towel.

Step 4: Assemble Tacos

Place a few strips of carne asada on each tortilla. Top with diced onions, fresh cilantro, and a squeeze of lime. Serve immediately with your favorite salsa on the side.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Meat Tip: For extra tender carne asada, score the meat against the grain before marinating.

Tortilla Trick: Double up tortillas per taco to prevent breaking - this is how they're served in Mexico!

Variation: Try with al pastor (marinated pork) or carnitas (braised pork) instead of carne asada.

Vegetarian Option: Substitute grilled portobello mushrooms or cauliflower for the meat.
