Vegetable Dalia

30 mins Serves 2 Easy
4.3 (127 reviews)

A wholesome and nutritious breakfast made with broken wheat (dalia), mixed vegetables and aromatic spices. This healthy one-pot meal is packed with fiber and perfect for a light yet satisfying start to your day.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup broken wheat (dalia)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped

Vegetables:

  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste

For Garnish:

  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

Step 1: Roast Dalia

Dry roast dalia in a pan for 2-3 minutes until fragrant. Set aside.

Step 2: Temper Spices

Heat ghee in pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds, then sauté onions until golden.

Step 3: Cook Vegetables

Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add all spices and mixed vegetables. Sauté for 2 minutes.

Step 4: Pressure Cook

Add roasted dalia and water. Pressure cook for 3 whistles on medium heat.

Step 5: Serve

Garnish with coriander leaves and lemon juice. Serve hot with yogurt.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Variation: Add grated coconut for South Indian flavor or garam masala for North Indian touch.

Healthy Twist: Use quinoa instead of dalia for protein-rich version.

Quick Tip: Soak dalia for 15 minutes to reduce cooking time.

Storage: Refrigerate for up to 2 days. Reheat with little water.
