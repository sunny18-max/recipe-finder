Kachori

1 hour 15 mins Serves 6 Medium
4.8 (325 reviews)

Flaky, deep-fried pastry stuffed with spicy lentil or pea filling. A popular North Indian snack often served with potato curry and chutneys.

Ingredients

For Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water as needed

For Filling:

  • 1 cup yellow moong dal
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp amchur
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Dough

Mix flour, ghee and salt. Add water to make stiff dough. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Make Filling

Soak dal for 2 hours. Drain and grind coarsely. Heat oil, add spices and dal. Cook until dry. Cool.

Step 3: Shape Kachoris

Divide dough into balls. Flatten, add filling, seal and roll into 3" discs.

Step 4: Fry Kachoris

Deep fry on medium heat until golden brown and puffed up. Drain on paper towels.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Dough Tip: The dough should be stiffer than roti dough for crisp samosas.

Frying Secret: Maintain medium heat - too hot will brown outside before inside cooks.

Variation: Try minced meat or paneer fillings for non-veg versions.

Storage: Freeze uncooked samosas for up to a month.
