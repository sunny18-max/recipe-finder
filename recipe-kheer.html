Rice Kheer

1 hour Serves 6 Easy
4.6 (289 reviews)

Creamy, aromatic rice pudding made with basmati rice, milk and flavored with cardamom, saffron and nuts. This traditional Indian dessert is perfect for festivals and special occasions.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup basmati rice
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10-12 saffron strands

For Garnish:

  • 2 tbsp chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • 1 tbsp raisins
  • 1 tsp rose petals (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Soak Rice

Wash and soak rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Cook Rice

In heavy-bottomed pan, boil milk. Add soaked rice and simmer on low heat, stirring frequently.

Step 3: Reduce Milk

Cook until rice is soft and milk reduces to half its volume (about 30-40 minutes). Stir occasionally.

Step 4: Add Flavors

Add sugar, cardamom and saffron. Cook for another 5-10 minutes until desired consistency.

Step 5: Garnish & Serve

Garnish with nuts and raisins. Serve warm or chilled.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Rice Tip: Use aged basmati rice for best flavor and texture.

Slow Cooking: Cook on lowest flame for creamiest kheer.

Variations: Add condensed milk for richer version or coconut milk for vegan option.

Serving Suggestion: Tastes best when chilled for 2-3 hours.
