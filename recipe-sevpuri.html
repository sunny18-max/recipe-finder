Sev Puri

30 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian
4.9 (512 reviews)

A popular Mumbai street food, Sev Puri is an explosion of flavors and textures - crispy puris topped with spicy potatoes, tangy chutneys, crunchy sev, and fresh veggies.

Ingredients

For Base:

  • 20-25 flat puris (papdis)
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and diced
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro

For Chutneys:

  • 1/2 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/4 cup mint-coriander chutney
  • 1 tbsp garlic chutney (optional)

For Toppings:

  • 1 cup sev (thin gram flour noodles)
  • 1/4 cup nylon sev (for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

Boil and dice potatoes. Chop all vegetables finely. Keep chutneys ready.

Step 2: Assemble Base

Arrange puris on serving plate. Top each puri with potatoes and chickpeas. Sprinkle onions and tomatoes.

Step 3: Add Chutneys

Drizzle tamarind chutney generously over each puri. Add mint-coriander chutney. Add garlic chutney if using.

Step 4: Add Toppings

Sprinkle chaat masala and red chili powder. Generously cover with sev. Garnish with nylon sev, pomegranate seeds and cilantro.

Step 5: Serve Immediately

Serve immediately before puris get soggy.

Chef's Notes

Puris: Store-bought papdis work fine, but homemade ones taste better.

Chutneys: Adjust sweetness and spice levels according to taste.

Variations: Add sprouts or grated beetroot for healthier version.

Serving: Assemble just before eating to maintain crispness.
