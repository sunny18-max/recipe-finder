Ratatouille

1.5 hours Serves 6 Vegan
4.7 (324 reviews)

Classic Provençal vegetable stew featuring eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and tomatoes in a rich herbed sauce. Our version includes both the traditional rustic preparation and the elegant confit byaldi presentation made famous by the animated film, allowing you to choose your preferred style.

Ingredients

Main Vegetables:

  • 1 large eggplant, diced
  • 2 zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 4 tomatoes, diced

For Sauce:

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp herbes de Provence

For Confit Byaldi (Optional):

  • 2 Roma tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, thinly sliced
  • 1 eggplant, thinly sliced

Instructions

Step 1: Salt Vegetables

Toss eggplant with salt and let drain in colander 30 minutes. Rinse and pat dry.

Step 2: Make Sauce

Sauté onion in olive oil until soft. Add garlic and herbes de Provence, cook 1 minute. Add tomatoes and simmer 10 minutes.

Step 3: Cook Vegetables

In separate pans, sauté each vegetable until slightly softened. Combine all with sauce.

Step 4: Simmer

Cook uncovered over low heat 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Confit Byaldi Option:

Spread sauce in baking dish. Arrange sliced vegetables concentrically over sauce. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes covered, then 15 minutes uncovered.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Texture Tip: Cook vegetables separately for best texture.

Make Ahead: Tastes even better the next day as flavors meld.

Serving Suggestions: Excellent with crusty bread or over polenta.

Shortcut: Use pre-sliced vegetables from salad bar for confit byaldi.
