Classic Panna Cotta

6 hours (includes setting) Serves 6 Vegetarian
4.6 (298 reviews)

The iconic Italian dessert of sweetened cream thickened with gelatin and molded into a silky, wobbly custard. This classic version flavored with vanilla bean has a delicate texture that melts in your mouth, traditionally served with berry coulis or caramel sauce.

Ingredients

For Panna Cotta:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean, split lengthwise
  • 2½ tsp powdered gelatin

For Serving:

  • Mixed fresh berries
  • Berry coulis or caramel sauce
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Bloom Gelatin

Sprinkle gelatin over 3 tbsp cold water in small bowl. Let stand 5-10 mins to soften.

Step 2: Infuse Cream

In saucepan, combine cream, milk and sugar. Scrape vanilla seeds into mixture and add pod. Heat until steaming but not boiling.

Step 3: Add Gelatin

Remove vanilla pod. Add bloomed gelatin to warm cream mixture and stir until completely dissolved.

Step 4: Pour and Chill

Strain mixture into 6 ramekins or glasses. Refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight until set.

Step 5: Serve

To unmold, dip ramekins briefly in hot water and invert onto plates. Or serve in glasses topped with berries and sauce.

Video Tutorial

Chef's Notes

Gelatin Tip: Don't boil after adding gelatin to maintain setting power.

Vegetarian Option: Use agar-agar instead of gelatin.

Flavor Variations: Infuse with citrus zest, lavender or coffee.

Make Ahead: Keeps well refrigerated for 3 days.
